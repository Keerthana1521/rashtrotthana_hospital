Department of OBG:
The OBG department at Rashtrotthana hospital is driven by the vision of “Mathruswasthyam Lokaswasthyam” - Mother’s Health is World’s Health. Highly experienced & dedicated team of OBGYN available at JMRH provide high quality service at affordable prices. The team of OBGYN run super speciality services through specialized clinics involving many specialities available under one roof jointly in a single clinic for comprehensive care of women striving to achieve the vision.

Highlights of OBG Department at JMRH:
Emphasis on Vaginal Delivery. Comprehensive maternity packages from 3rd month to Delivery. Post Delivery Home care package - Ayurvedic treatment with Abhyanaga @ the comfort of your home. Rs. 25,000/- for 10 sessions. Yoga package for expecting mother’s covering both pre & post delivery benefits.
