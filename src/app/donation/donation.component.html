About Us

Donate today to give hope and change lives!

The noble aim of Rashtrotthana Hospital is to provide healthcare that nurtures both body and spirit. Guided by our motto, “Sarve Santu Niramaya,” meaning “May All Be Healthy,” we are committed to ensuring that every individual receives the care they need, regardless of their circumstances. Your donation to Rashtrotthana Hospital directly supports this mission, enabling us to offer vital medical treatments and services to those in need. Thank you for being a part of our journey to create a healthier and more equitable world.

Touching Lives!

Clinic on Wheels

Our mobile healthcare initiative serves remote communities, aiming to bridge healthcare gaps and provide preventive care & treatment to those with limited access to medical services

Poor Patient Service Fund

Dedicated to support indigent patients, this fund helps cover a portion of hospitalization expenses, ensuring equitable healthcare access.

Old Age Homes

Tailored healthcare services cater to the specific needs of elderly residents, acknowledging and addressing their unique health requirements.

School Health Program

Engaging in comprehensive health programs within schools fosters a culture of wellness among students, promoting their overall health.

Orphanage Support

Extending healthcare services to children in orphanages ensures access to fundamental healthcare facilities for those in need.

Community Medical Camps

Our commitment to community welfare is evident in our weekly free medical camps, strategically reaching urban and rural areas, aiming to provide essential healthcare services.
