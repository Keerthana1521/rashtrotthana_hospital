Endocrinology

Rashtrotthana Hospital's Endocrinology Department specializes in treating a wide range of disorders related to the endocrine glands, encompassing variations in physical growth and metabolic conditions such as diabetes. Our team of endocrinologists is highly experienced in managing various endocrine disorders in both adolescents and adults, providing personalized care and effective treatment plans for each patient.

We treat a wide range of endocrine disorders, including but not limited to:

  • Thyroid disorders (including carcinoma/tumors, hyperthyroidism, and hypothyroidism)
  • Pituitary disorders (including hypopituitarism)
  • Adrenal gland disorders
  • Diabetes (Type 1 and Type 2)
  • Childhood obesity
  • Parathyroid issues
  • Bone and mineral disorders
  • Lipid disorder
