About Us

Human Health Care Service
Through State-Of-The-Art-
Infrastructure.
Jayadev Memorial Rashtrotthana Hospital & Research Centre is a 162 Bed Integrated Multi- Specialty Tertiary Care Hospital with world class health care facilities. Jayadev Memorial Rashtrotthana Hospital & Research Centre is a Unit of Rashtrotthana trust. Our renowned doctors and specialists in the field of Modern Medicine, Ayurveda, Homeopathy, Yoga & Naturopathy provide expert care with a holistic approach.
The guiding force behind Rashtrotthana Parishat, Sri M C Jayadev, envisioned, nurtured and mentored the organisation from inception. The very embodiment of duty blended with nationalism, his life-long adherence to 'One Life One Mission' was unparalleled. Rashtrotthana Parishat is moving towards its objective of building a prosperous and healthy society by virtue of the durable foundation laid by him.
