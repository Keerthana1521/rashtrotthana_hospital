What is Total Knee Replacement (TKR)?
Total Knee Replacement (TKR) is a surgical procedure designed to relieve knee pain and improve function by replacing the damaged parts of the knee joint with artificial components. This surgery involves removing damaged cartilage and bone from the knee and replacing it with prosthetic implants made of metal, high-grade plastics and polymers. TKR surgery is a highly effective solution for those suffering from severe knee pain, helping them regain mobility and improve their quality of life.

At Rashtrotthana Hospital, we specialize in addressing the underlying knee pain causes to provide comprehensive care for patients requiring knee replacement surgery.
Why Choose Total Knee Replacement?
TKR surgery is typically recommended for individuals who experience chronic knee pain that interferes with daily activities and does not respond to nonsurgical treatments. While it is most commonly performed on patients aged 60 and older, younger patients with severe knee damage due to arthritis or injury can also benefit from the procedure. Candidates for TKR often suffer from:

  • Severe knee pain that limits daily activities such as walking, climbing stairs, and sitting or rising from chairs.
  • Moderate or severe knee pain while resting, both day and night.
  • Chronic knee inflammation and swelling that doesn’t improve with rest or medications.
  • Knee deformity, where there is a noticeable arching in or out of the knee.
  • No significant relief from other knee pain treatments such as physical therapy, medications, or injections.
  • Various knee pain causes such as osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, post-traumatic arthritis, injury and overuse.
Why is TKR Surgery Recommended?
TKR surgery is recommended when other treatments for knee pain have failed to provide relief. This includes:

Knee Pain Causes: Osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, post-traumatic arthritis, injury, overuse.

Knee Pain Treatment: Physical therapy, medications, lifestyle modifications, surgery.

Knee Pain Reasons: Degenerative joint disease, injuries, aging, overuse.

By replacing the damaged knee joint with a prosthetic one, TKR surgery can effectively reduce pain, improve function, and enhance the overall quality of life for patients with severe knee issues.
