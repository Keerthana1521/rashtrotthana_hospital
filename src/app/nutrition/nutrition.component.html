Nutrition & Dietetics
The dietetic department at Jayadev Memorial Rashtrotthana Hospital provides high quality food & nutrition service in more than a professional way, by giving personalized attention to each particular need and expectation of each patient.
Our dieticians are highly professional and experienced to establish nutritional needs and to recommend appropriate nutritional treatment for both inpatient & outpatients and to manage their disease and optimize their health.
Services:
  • Nutritional assessment and screening of both inpatients and outpatients.
  • Re-assessment of patients at defined intervals.
  • Coordinate with doctors and other healthcare professionals in providing medical and nutritional support to patients.
  • Planning and monitoring the diets of in and out patients with respect to their health status.
  • Diet counseling and education of patients.
