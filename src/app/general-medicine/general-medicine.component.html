Internal Medicine

Internal Medicine dept also provides advanced medical treatment for Infectious diseases, metabolic disorders, lifestyle diseases & co-management of respiratory, gastro, cardiac, nephrology & neuro cases. For handling critical medical cases - dedicated 6 bedded Medical ICU is available & a High dependency unit (HDU) is also available for patients requiring continuous monitoring. Internal Medicine, also referred to as Internal Medicine, is a branch of medicine that deals with the prevention, diagnosis and the non-surgical treatments of diseases that are related to the internal organs.
